About ReadON

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team behind ReadON Library. We're dedicated to fostering a love for reading and learning in our community.

Our Mission & Vision

At ReadON, our mission is to provide free and equitable access to information, ideas, and knowledge through books, resources, and services that inform, educate, and entertain.

We envision a community where everyone has the tools and opportunities to learn, grow, and connect through the power of reading. Our library serves as a cornerstone for education, innovation, and cultural enrichment.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to promoting literacy, encouraging lifelong learning, and serving as a welcoming space for all members of our community.

Our Values

Accessibility

We believe knowledge should be available to everyone, regardless of background, age, or ability.

Community

We serve as a gathering place that strengthens community bonds through shared learning experiences.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our services and resources to meet the changing needs of our patrons.

Excellence

We are dedicated to delivering nothing less than excellence in everything we do and setting new industry standards.
